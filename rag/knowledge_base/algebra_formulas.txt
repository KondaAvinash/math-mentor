# Algebra Formulas & Identities

## Arithmetic Progression (AP)
- nth term: a_n = a + (n-1)d
- nth term from end: a_n = l - (n-1)d  where l = last term
- Sum of n terms: S_n = n/2 * (2a + (n-1)d) = n/2 * (a + l)
- Number of terms: n = (l - a)/d + 1
- Middle term: a + (n-1)/2 * d  (when n is odd)

## Geometric Progression (GP)
- nth term: a_n = a * r^(n-1)
- Sum of n terms: S_n = a(r^n - 1)/(r - 1)  for r ≠ 1
- Sum to infinity: S = a/(1-r)  for |r| < 1
- Geometric mean: GM = sqrt(a*b)

## Quadratic Equations
- Roots: x = (-b ± sqrt(b²-4ac)) / 2a
- Sum of roots: α + β = -b/a
- Product of roots: αβ = c/a
- Discriminant: D = b²-4ac
  - D > 0: two distinct real roots
  - D = 0: two equal real roots
  - D < 0: two complex roots

## Binomial Theorem
- (a+b)^n = Σ C(n,r) * a^(n-r) * b^r
- General term: T_(r+1) = C(n,r) * a^(n-r) * b^r
- Middle term: T_(n/2 + 1) when n is even

## Algebraic Identities
- (a+b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
- (a-b)² = a² - 2ab + b²
- (a+b)³ = a³ + 3a²b + 3ab² + b³
- a²-b² = (a+b)(a-b)
- a³-b³ = (a-b)(a²+ab+b²)
- a³+b³ = (a+b)(a²-ab+b²)

## Logarithms
- log(mn) = log m + log n
- log(m/n) = log m - log n
- log(m^n) = n log m
- log_a(b) = log(b)/log(a)  (change of base)
- log_a(a) = 1
- log_a(1) = 0

## Sequences and Series
- Sum of first n natural numbers: n(n+1)/2
- Sum of squares: n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
- Sum of cubes: [n(n+1)/2]²
- Arithmetic mean: AM = (a+b)/2
- Harmonic mean: HM = 2ab/(a+b)
- AM ≥ GM ≥ HM (for positive numbers)
